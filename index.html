    OpenNOMS is a collaboration between the Metropolitan Airports Commission and Farallon Geographics to develop an Open Source Airport Noise and Operations Management System. We would love to invite other airports and developers to work with us on this system. Please contact bitner@macnoise.com to find out more about what we are doing.
